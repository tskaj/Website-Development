INDIVIDUAL PSYCHOTHERAPY

Whether you are experiencing challenges associated with mental health or are looking at ways for personal growth and development, meet me one on one in a safe, confidential and non-judgmental environment.

Some of the benefits of individual psychotherapy include:

  • Self-awareness and personal growth
  • Improved self esteem
  • Reduced anxiety
  • Processing of Trauma
  • Positive coping skills
  • Improved communication and relationships
