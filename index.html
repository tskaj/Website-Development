Frequently Asked Questions

Psychotherapy is a type of treatment that aims to help individuals overcome their emotional and mental health problems. It involves talking with a trained therapist who can provide guidance and support in a safe and non-judgmental environment.
A typical psychotherapy session lasts around 50 minutes. However, the exact duration may vary depending on the individual needs and goals of the client.
The frequency of psychotherapy sessions may vary depending on the individual needs and goals of the client. Typically, clients attend one session per week, but some may attend more or less frequently depending on their circumstances.
